About Us

Founded in 1983, “FCO” (Woman to Woman) is a successor to the feminist women of Haifa who fought for women’s rights in the 1970s.

We are a community of women acting out of a desire to maintain partnership and understanding among women in Israel-Palestine, regardless of religion, nationality, ethnic origin, sexual preference, or socioeconomic status.

We are the oldest feminist center in Israel-Palestine and a leading voice in the struggle for women’s rights in the country.

Our mission is to advance the status and rights of women and girls, and to promote peace, security, and socio-economic justice from a feminist perspective.

Climate change affects women from marginalized communities more than others because they already face difficult circumstances. They may have less access to resources and be more vulnerable to environmental changes like floods or droughts. However, these women also have valuable knowledge and skills to cope with climate challenges. Sadly, they are often left out of important decisions about how to tackle climate change. To make a positive difference, we need to support and involve these women in finding solutions for a better and fairer future.
